Xchange App

Scope

With the help of this application , users can check EUR , USD , TRY and GBP exchange rates based on RON . A wide group of users can benefit from this application , including tourists , businessmen and stock traders . The interface was designed by using Fluid UI for touchscreen mobile phones and it is easy to use .

The application is trustful as it uses up-to-date values . And requires internet connection at least once to update values . The application does not require any username or password to access .

The date is shown above the exchange rates , in the same page . Names of the developers can be found by tapping “About Us” button .
